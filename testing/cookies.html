What are cookies?

A "cookie" is a small text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. The information stored can be used to "remember" your actions or preferences, to identify you as a unique visitor, and so on.


How we use cookies

All cookies currently saved when using our website are from the services that we use. These include:

  • Cloudflare, for web service reliability.
    You can read their policy here: https://www.cloudflare.com/cookie-policy/

We do not have direct control over the way these services use the cookies they save.


How to delete/control cookies

You can delete any cookies already on your computer yourself, and most browsers allow you to prevent them from being saved at all. If you do that, however, many services will not work as intended. You can learn more about cookies at aboutcookies.org.
